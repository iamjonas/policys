# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 23 February 2026**

Siftr ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application operated by Jonas Persson, Sweden ("the data controller"). This privacy policy applies to users located in the **European Economic Area (EEA)**, where the app is available via the Apple App Store. It explains what personal data we collect, the legal basis for processing it, how it is used, and your rights under applicable law, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 2. Data We Collect and Legal Basis

### Email Address
- **Purpose:** Authentication via one-time passcode (OTP).  
- **Legal basis:** Performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))  

### Dietary Restrictions and Cuisine Preferences
- **Purpose:** Filtering recipes and personalising your experience.  
- **Legal basis:** Performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))  

### Allergy Information
- **Purpose:** Safety-critical filtering.  
- **Legal basis:** Explicit consent (GDPR Art. 9(2)(a))  

### Camera and Photos
- **Purpose:** Photographing fridge contents via AI.  
- **Legal basis:** Performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))  
- Photos are **never stored**.

### Scan Count
- **Purpose:** Enforcing free-tier usage limits.  
- **Legal basis:** Legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))  

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to provide and improve Siftr. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data for advertising. All processing is aimed at providing service to EEA users.

All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest. Reasonable technical and organisational measures are applied.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Siftr is not directed at children under 13. If a child has provided data, contact us to delete it.
